What is snowflakes ?

snowflakes is a must have for every snow-themed website. With this CSS based project you can let snowflakes fall down on your site pretty easily. However snowflakes is not quite ready for release yet but it will be here for you very soon. So be sure to check back here and get your own snowflakes for your website

snowflakes

There are dozens of free CSS snowflakes projects out there so why should i use this one ?

Because snowflakes is way more customizable than most of the other projects. And also you can use it without any CSS or JS knowledge and still get an awesome dynamic background for your website. Furthermore you will be able to customize every aspect of your snowflakes directly on this website and get your code instantly.
